# Product

## Intent

Nubel Store is the electronics commerce section of Nubel Systems, with a public catalog, WhatsApp purchase flow, customer Google accounts, internal operations dashboard, and a growing 3D printing quote workflow.

## Users

- Electronics buyers looking for components.
- Internal operators managing products, stock, and sales.
- Future 3D printing customers and manufacturers.

## Main Flows

- Search and inspect electronic products.
- Request products through WhatsApp.
- Sign in with Google as a customer.
- Manage products, sales, inventory, and stock movements.
- Upload STL files and request 3D printing quotes.
